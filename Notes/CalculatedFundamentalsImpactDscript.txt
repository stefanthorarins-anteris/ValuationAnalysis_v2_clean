## Classic
Current ratio => positive
P/E => Ambigeous
PEG => ?
Leverage (D/E) => ?
dividend yield => ?

## Non-standard
Big:
inventory increasing faster than sales => negative future earnings
increase in percentage change in gross margin relative to sales => positive future earnings

Medium:
increase in sales per employee => positive future earnings
increase in selling and administrative expanses relative to sales = negative future earnings

Aux:
higher effective tax rates => negative future earnings
increase in account receivables relative to sales => positive future earnings
